# Idea Stash

Don't let great ideas escape. Capture them all, organize them smart.

## What It Does

A lightning-fast idea capture app that works offline and organizes your thoughts using AI-powered tagging. Your ideas, secured locally.

## Features

- **Instant Capture** - One tap to jot down any idea. Voice, text, or photo. Works offline.
- **Smart Tags** - AI-powered organization finds connections between your ideas automatically.
- **Private by Default** - Your ideas, your device. No cloud sync unless you explicitly choose it.
