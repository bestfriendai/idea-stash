# IdeaStash Runbook

## Daily Operations

### Monitoring
- Check Expo dashboard for crashes
- Monitor app startup time

### Content Management
- Sample ideas included on first launch
- Ideas persist locally via AsyncStorage

## Troubleshooting

### "No ideas found"
- Normal on first launch
- Sample ideas appear after app starts

### App crashes
- Clear cache and reinstall
- Check `rm -rf node_modules && npm install`
